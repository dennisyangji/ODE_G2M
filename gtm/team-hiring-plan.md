## Hiring Roadmap

### Month 1: Head of Growth / Marketing (P0 — Hire #7)

**Why first**: This person builds the entire PLG engine. Without them, $400K PLG revenue won't happen. Every month of delay costs ~$30K in lost pipeline.

**Profile:**
- 5-8 years in B2B SaaS marketing, ideally in dev tools or engineering software
- Hands-on: can write content, run ads, set up analytics, and manage CRM
- Experience with PLG motions (freemium conversion, product-led onboarding)
- Technical enough to understand CAD/CAE/simulation messaging
- Comfortable being a team of one initially, building the function from scratch

**Responsibilities (M1-M6):**
- Own website redesign and brand launch
- Build and execute content engine (2-4 posts/week)
- Set up and manage paid acquisition (LinkedIn + Google)
- Configure CRM (HubSpot), analytics (Mixpanel), email (Customer.io)
- Manage $300K marketing budget
- Report on pipeline, CAC, conversion metrics weekly

**Compensation:**
- Base: $100K - $120K
- Equity: 0.5% - 1.0% (early GTM hire at seed stage)
- Performance bonus: tied to MQL targets and ARR milestones
- Fully loaded (benefits, tools): $120K - $150K

**Where to find:**
- LinkedIn (B2B SaaS marketing leaders at 20-50 person startups)
- Product Marketing Alliance / Pavilion communities
- Engineering tool company alumni (Autodesk, PTC, MathWorks, ANSYS)
- Y Combinator's Work at a Startup board

---

### Month 3: Account Executive — Enterprise (P0 — Hire #8)

**Why M3**: Enterprise sales cycles are 3-6 months. Hiring at M3 means first deals close by M6-M9, hitting the SLG revenue targets. CEO handles initial enterprise outreach M1-M3.

**Profile:**
- 3-5 years B2B enterprise sales, ideally selling to engineering or R&D teams
- Experience selling $30K-$100K+ ACV deals to VP Engineering / CTO
- Comfortable with technical demos (can learn Modelica basics)
- Consultative sales approach, not transactional
- DACH region experience is a plus (for European enterprise market)

**Responsibilities:**
- Own the enterprise sales pipeline from SQL to close
- Run discovery calls, demos, pilots, and contract negotiations
- Work with CEO on top 10 target accounts
- Build relationships at aerospace, automotive, and energy companies
- Manage 20-30 active opportunities at any time
- Report weekly on pipeline, forecast, and deal progress

**Compensation:**
- Base: $70K - $80K
- OTE (On-Target Earnings): $130K - $160K (50/50 split)
- Commission: 10-12% of ACV on new business
- Equity: 0.25% - 0.5%
- Fully loaded: $100K - $130K (base + estimated commission Year 1)

**Quota:**
- Year 1 (prorated, M3-M12): $400K - $500K pipeline, $200K - $300K closed
- Ramp: M3-M5 = learning, M6+ = quota-carrying

### Month 6: SDR / BDR — Outbound (P1 — Hire #10)

**Why M6**: By M6, the AE has validated the enterprise sales process and knows what a qualified lead looks like. The SDR accelerates pipeline generation so the AE can focus on closing.

**Profile:**
- 1-3 years in B2B outbound sales development
- Experience with engineering or technical audience outreach
- Comfortable with cold email, LinkedIn outreach, and phone

- Energy and resilience — outbound is a volume game

**Responsibilities:**
- Execute ABM campaigns against top 50 enterprise target accounts
- 50-80 outreach activities/day (email, LinkedIn, phone)
- Qualify leads using ICP framework and BANT criteria
- Book 15-20 discovery calls/month for AE
- Manage enterprise lead nurture sequences
- Attend conferences and events for lead capture

**Compensation:**
- Base: $45K - $55K
- OTE: $70K - $85K
- Bonus: $200-300 per qualified meeting booked
- Equity: 0.1% - 0.2%
- Fully loaded: $60K - $80K

### Month 8: Customer Success / Solutions Engineer (P2 — Hire #11)

**Why M8**: By M8, there are 100+ paid users and 5+ enterprise pilots. Churn prevention and expansion become critical. This person ensures customers succeed and expand.

**Compensation:**

## Hiring Decision Framework

### When to Accelerate Hiring
- Free sign-ups exceeding 150/month (Growth Lead overwhelmed → hire Content/DevRel earlier)
- Enterprise pipeline >$500K with only 1 AE → hire second AE or promote SDR
- Monthly churn >5% among paid users → hire CS earlier
- Revenue ahead of plan by >20% → accelerate all hires by 1-2 months

### When to Delay Hiring
- Free sign-ups below 50/month by M3 → delay SDR, focus on PLG first
- Zero enterprise pilots by M5 → delay SDR, re-evaluate enterprise positioning
- Marketing spend CAC above $1,500 blended → optimize channels before adding people
- Runway drops below 12 months → freeze non-critical hires, extend with contractors

### Contractors vs Full-Time

| Function | Full-Time | Contractor Alternative |
|----------|-----------|----------------------|
| Head of Growth | Yes — needs to own strategy | No — too strategic |
| Account Executive | Yes — relationship-driven | Fractional VP Sales for M1-M3 coaching |
| Content/DevRel | Yes — consistency matters | Freelance writers for blog ($200-500/post) |
| SDR | Yes, but can start with contractor | Outsourced SDR agency ($3-5K/month) |
| Customer Success | Can start part-time | Contract CSM initially |
