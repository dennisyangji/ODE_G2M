## Key Assumptions & Risks

### Assumptions

1. Product achieves minimum viable quality for paid users by M2.
2. Free tier conversion rate stabilizes at 5-8% by M4.
3. Enterprise sales cycle averages 6 months (first enterprise revenue by M4-M6).
4. Monthly churn rate: 3-5% for PLG users, <1% for SLG accounts.
5. Net revenue retention reaches 110%+ by M9 through usage expansion.
6. Content SEO begins driving meaningful organic traffic by M4-M5.

### Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Slow enterprise sales | Medium | High | Over-invest in PLG to maintain revenue growth while enterprise pipeline matures |
| High PLG churn | Medium | Medium | Activation-focused onboarding, in-app guidance, early intervention triggers |
| Paid channel inefficiency | Low-Medium | Medium | Start small, test rigorously, reallocate budget monthly to highest-performing channels |
| Competitor response | Low | High | Build AI-native differentiation that incumbents cannot quickly replicate |
| Budget overrun | Low | Medium | Contingency fund, monthly budget reviews, quarterly reforecasting |
