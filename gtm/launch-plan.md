## AI-Native Execution Principles

> Every phase runs in parallel. AI agents do first drafts of everything.

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| AI drafts, humans approve | No human writes a cold first draft. Claude/AI tools draft; team refines in <15 min |
| Measure AI leverage weekly | Output per person tracked; "AI ratio" reported to CEO every Monday |
| Ship in days, not weeks | Landing page in 2 days (Cursor + Framer). Blog post in 2 hours (Claude). |
| Channel multiplies headcount | 1 Sales Manager + 3 partners = 4× coverage without hiring |
