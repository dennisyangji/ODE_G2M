# ODE Channel Strategy

## Channel Overview

ODE's channel strategy serves two growth engines: PLG (developer/engineer-led) and SLG (enterprise sales-led). Each channel is prioritized by ICP alignment, CAC efficiency, and scalability.

## Detailed Channel Plans

### 1. Website & SEO (PLG Foundation)

**Objective**: Convert 3%+ of visitors to free sign-ups

**Tactics:**
- Landing page optimized for "AI engineering platform", "AI CAD", "Modelica AI"
- Interactive AI demo embedded on homepage (zero-friction try)
- SEO-optimized pages for each tool (Component, Paper, System, Vision, Geometry, Interaction)
- Comparison pages: "ODE vs Autodesk Fusion", "ODE vs SimScale"
- Use case pages by industry (aerospace, automotive, energy, robotics)

**Keywords (Priority):**
| Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | Intent |
|---------|-------:|:----------:|--------|
| AI CAD software | 1,200 | Medium | High |
| AI engineering platform | 800 | Low | High |
| Modelica simulation online | 500 | Low | High |
| cloud engineering tools | 2,000 | High | Medium |
| browser-based CAD | 1,500 | Medium | High |
| AI-powered simulation | 600 | Low | High |

### 2. Content Marketing / Technical Blog

**Objective**: 10,000 organic visits/month by Q4

**Content Pillars:**
1. **AI-Native Engineering** — thought leadership on AI transforming design
2. **Modelica + AI** — technical deep dives on the LLM + equation-based synergy
3. **Engineering Tutorials** — hands-on guides using ODE
4. **Industry Insights** — trends in aerospace, automotive, energy engineering

**Publishing Cadence:** 2 blog posts/week, 1 video/week, 1 whitepaper/quarter

### 3. LinkedIn (Organic + Paid)

**Organic Strategy:**
- Founder-led content: 3 posts/week (AI in engineering, product updates, industry insights)
- Company page: 5 posts/week (blog shares, demos, customer stories)
- Employee advocacy: team shares and comments

**Paid Strategy:**
- Sponsored content targeting: Engineering Managers, VP Engineering, CTO
- Industries: Aerospace, Automotive, Manufacturing, Energy
- Company sizes: 500+ employees (enterprise), 10-500 (mid-market)
- Formats: Single image, video (demo clips), carousel (feature highlights)
- Budget: $4,000/month, targeting $15-25 CPL for enterprise leads

### 4. Google Ads

**Objective**: Capture high-intent search traffic

**Campaign Structure:**
- Brand: "ODE engineering", "Orthogonal Design Engineering"
- Competitor: "Autodesk alternative", "SimScale alternative"
- Category: "AI CAD software", "cloud engineering platform"
- Feature: "Modelica simulation online", "AI-powered design tool"

**Budget**: $2,500/month, targeting <$50 CPC for high-intent terms

### 5. GitHub & Open Source

**Objective**: Build developer trust and community

**Tactics:**
- Maintain Modelica MCP Server as open-source flagship
- Regular releases with changelog and community engagement
- Sponsor relevant open-source Modelica projects
- Create starter templates and example models
- Engage in Modelica Association forums

### 6. Conferences & Events

**Priority Events:**
| Event | When | Budget | Expected Outcome |
|-------|------|-------:|-----------------|
| Modelica Conference | Biennial | $8,000 | 20+ enterprise leads, 5 pilots |
| Engineering summit (regional) | H2 | $8,000 | 15+ enterprise leads |
| Hosted webinar series | Monthly | $500/mo | 50 MQLs/quarter |
| University guest lectures | Quarterly | $1,000 | Academic adoption |

### 7. Partnerships

**Partner Types:**
- **System Integrators**: Co-sell into enterprise accounts, revenue share
- **Engineering Consultancies**: Use ODE for client projects, become champions
- **University Programs**: Academic licenses, research collaborations
- **Technology Partners**: Modelica Association, FMI standard body
