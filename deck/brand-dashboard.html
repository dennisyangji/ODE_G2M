Structural Moat — Architecture Gap
Dassault's CATIA/SIMULIA architecture was built in the 1990s. Binary proprietary formats are opaque to large language models. Rebuilding for AI would destroy backwards compatibility — impossible for a company protecting $5B revenue and multi-year enterprise contracts.

The moat: Modelica is equation-based and inherently machine-readable. LLMs can parse, generate, and reason about physics equations natively. This is not a feature gap — it is an architecture gap that cannot be closed by bolt-on AI features.
